CVD PROGRAM

Title: Coordinated Vulnerability Disclosure Program

1. Introduction
We value the security of our payment gateway and encourage responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities. This program outlines the guidelines for reporting potential security issues.

2. Scope
This program covers all systems and services provided by our payment gateway company.

3. Reporting a Vulnerability
- Describe the vulnerability clearly and concisely.
- Include steps to reproduce and any relevant logs or screenshots.
- Do not exploit the vulnerability or disclose it to others.

4. Our Commitment
- We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 3 business days.
- We will keep you informed of the progress and resolution.
- We will not take legal action against you if you follow this policy.

5. Disclosure
Once the issue is resolved, we will disclose the vulnerability and our response to the reporter.

6. Contact
Please send reports to [security@paymentgateway.com](mailto:security@paymentgateway.com)

This program is subject to change without notice.
